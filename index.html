3D-Printed Prosthetic Hand

An open-source, servo-actuated prosthetic hand designed for accessibility, education, and real-world functionality. Built using affordable 3D printing technology and programmable microcontrollers.

Material
PLA/ABS 3D-printed components with nylon tendon wires
Weight
~400-500g (depending on configuration)
Actuation
5 servo motors with tendon-driven finger control
Features
Modular design, customizable grip patterns, Arduino-compatible programming
